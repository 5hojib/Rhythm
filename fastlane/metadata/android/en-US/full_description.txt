<p><i>Rhythm</i> is a music player for Android that delivers a seamless audio experience with a beautiful Material You design. Enjoy your music collection with advanced playback features, intuitive controls, and comprehensive audio format support.</p><p><b>Note:</b> The app includes an optional update checker. Be aware that installing updates from Github directly bypasses the extra screening applied at repositories like F-Droid and IzzyOnDroid.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li><b>🎨 Modern UI:</b> Built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3 design</li><li><b>🎵 Comprehensive Audio Support:</b> Plays various formats including MP3, AAC, FLAC, OGG, WAV</li><li><b>📱 Android Support:</b> Targets Android API 34 with compatibility down to Android 8.0</li><li><b>🎯 Media3 Integration:</b> Robust media playback using ExoPlayer</li><li><b>📂 Advanced Playlist Management:</b> Create, edit, and organize playlists</li><li><b>🔄 Background Playback:</b> Continuous playback with foreground service</li><li><b>🎨 Dynamic Theming:</b> Material You/Monet theming and fluid animations</li><li><b>🔊 Smart Audio:</b> Gapless playback, crossfade, audio normalization</li><li><b>🎵 Advanced Features:</b> Equalizer, high-quality mode, replay gain</li><li><b>🔊 Output Control:</b> Seamless audio device and Bluetooth switching</li><li><b>📊 Personalization:</b> Listening stats, mood-based playlists</li><li><b>🌐 Online Integration:</b> Automatic updates, online lyrics</li><li><b>🔄 Auto-Resume:</b> Remembers playback state and queue</li><li><b>🎨 Adaptive UI:</b> Beautiful transitions and responsive design</li></ul>
